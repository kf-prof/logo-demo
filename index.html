Cast Iron Press logo demonstration - hover to select.
disclaimer: SVG's tend to be higher quality than PNG's, but I have attached both in the folder because they have their independ uses. SVG's use mathematic equations to achieve pixel-perfect edges, meaning no pixelation upon zooming in and infinite scaleability. PNG's however, due to their rasterized nature, have limited pixels that will be visible at larger scales. The PNGs in the folders are at print-quality 300ppi, but for digital settings (and whenever possible in use), I recommend sticking with the SVGs. Happy logo'ing!
